Homemade Pasta from Scratch

Making pasta dough by hand is easier than you think and requires just three ingredients. The result is tender, flavorful pasta far superior to anything from a box.

Ingredients

  • 400g tipo 00 flour (plus extra for dusting)

  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional)
Hands-on: 30 min Total: 1 hour Difficulty: Medium
